About us

Waituhi Watercress is situated on an orchard 20 km from Gisborne. As well as Hydroponic Watercress we also grow Avocados and sometimes sweetcorn.

History

Our Orchard has been in the family for 3 generations and over the years have produced various varieties of apples, grapes, kiwifruit and avocadoes, to name a few crops. In 2000 we decided to diversify and started experimenting with growing outdoor gravel culture Hydroponic Watercress. Demand quickly exceeded supply and we had to expand our operation.
